## **Handling Messy Human Language (Core NLP Challenge)**

* **Problem:** Real messages contain slang, typos, panic text
  *(e.g., “omg fire halp plss at sec 62”)*

* **Reality:** Keyword-based systems fail.

* **Solution:**

  * Use LLMs (Gemini / Hugging Face models)
  * Understand intent, not just keywords
  * Accurately classify severity (e.g., *Critical Fire*)

---

## **Geocoding Bottleneck (Hardest Challenge)**

* **Problem:** Extracted text ≠ map coordinates
  *(“Sector 62 Noida” → not usable directly)*

* **Solution:**

  * Use geocoding (Geopy + OpenStreetMap)
  * Convert → Latitude & Longitude
  * Example: *(28.62, 77.36)*

* **Hackathon Optimization:**

  * Pre-store 10–20 key locations
  * Ensures **zero latency demo**

---

## **Fake News & Spam Detection (Critical Layer)**

* **Problem:** Fake alerts can cause panic.

### **Multi-Layer Solution:**

1. **Clustering**

   * ≥3 reports
   * Within 2 km
   * Within 10 minutes

2. **Credibility Scoring**

   * Verified users → High trust
   * New accounts → Low trust

3. **Content Consistency**

   * Similar keywords + descriptions
   * Conflicts reduce confidence

4. **AI Spam Detection**

   * Detect bots, exaggeration, repeated patterns

5. **Human Approval Layer**

   * Critical alerts require manual confirmation

> “Sentinel does not react to noise—it waits for verified signal.”
